New or Updated in 2009

If only for the record, there follows a list of all pages that were either added to the Notes and Studies at this website during 2009 or had some meaningful update of their content. Note that some pages marked “draft” never have been returned to and very likely never will be.

Please, if you have benefited at all from any of these pages, or would like to see anything like the same volume of publication in 2010, then consider how you might support this site. Plainly there was a good book’s worth of new material published here in 2009. I should like to do something similar in 2010 and continue publishing it all as a free resource. However, I cannot do that without sponsorship, a grant, or a lot of individual support.
