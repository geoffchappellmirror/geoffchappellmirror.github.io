CL /ZBe

Syntax

The option is /ZBe only.

Relations

The /ZBe option is incompatible with /Za.

This option passes to the front-end C and C++ compilers (C1 and C1XX) only.

Behaviour

A side-effect of /ZBe within the front-end compilers is to enable /Ze.
