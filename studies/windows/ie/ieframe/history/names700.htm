IEFRAME 7.0 Named Exports

Given that IEFRAME exists as the extraction of Internet Explorer functionality from SHDOCVW, there’s no surprise that many of the functions that are exported from the original IEFRAME were previously exported from SHDOCVW. They remain exported from SHDOCVW but as forwards to IEFRAME. Most were undocumented as exports from SHDOCVW and this does not change just because they have moved to IEFRAME. Most new functions that IEFRAME exports by name, however, are documented.
