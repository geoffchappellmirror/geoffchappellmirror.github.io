IEFRAME 8.0 Named Exports

The column of Remarks and the usual colour-coding for documentation status await my receipt (on disc from an MSDN subscription) of a definitive SDK for Windows 7 to adopt as a formal reference edition of Microsoft’s documentation. Meanwhile, inspection of Microsoft’s online documentation suggests that all but a handful of functions that are newly exported by name for version 8.0 are documented, as is typical practice.

Many of the functions that are newly exported by name for version 8.0 have ordinals that break the auto-numbering of other named exports. This may be a sign that the functions were exported only by ordinal during development but were exported also by name when a decision was made to document them.
