CL /dlp

Syntax

positive:

/dlp

negative:

/dlp-

Relations

The positive and negative forms override each other, of course. Additionally, the /dlp option

  • weakly implies -D_CAP_PROFILING (as do /callcap, /cap and /fastcap)
  • requires /Zi or /Z7
  • is incompatible with /ZI

and the /dlp- option

  • overrides /D_CAP_PROFILING (as do /callcap-, /cap- and /fastcap-)
