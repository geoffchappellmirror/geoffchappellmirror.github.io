CL /Ob0

Syntax

The option is /Ob0 only.

Relations

The /Ob0 option is provided by CL as an initial option. Additionally, it

  • overrides /Ob1 and /Ob2, and is overridden by them
  • is weakly implied by /Od
  • is weakly overridden by /O1, /O2 and /Ox
