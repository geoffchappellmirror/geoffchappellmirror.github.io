CL /O2

Syntax

The option is /O2 only.

Relations

The /O2 option

  • weakly implies /GF, /Gy, /Ob2, /Og, /Oi, /Ot and /Oy
  • overrides /O1 and /Od, and is overridden by them
  • weakly overrides /GF, /Ob0, /Ob2, /Os and /Oy-
  • is incompatible with /GZ, /RTC1, /RTCc, /RTCs, /RTCu and /ZI
