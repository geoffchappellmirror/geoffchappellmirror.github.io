Exports Added For ADVAPI32 6.3

In terms just of its exported functions, version 6.3 of ADVAPI32 does something very rare: not only does it add none, it takes some away:

  • PsmActivateApplication
  • PsmAdjustActivationToken
  • PsmQueryBackgroundActivationType
  • PsmRegisterApplicationProcess
