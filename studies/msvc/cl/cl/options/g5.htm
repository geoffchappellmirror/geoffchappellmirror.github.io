CL /G5

Syntax

/G5

This option accepts no argument.

Relations

The /G5 option

  • weakly implies /D_M_IX86=500 for the front-end C and C++ compilers (C1 and C1XX)
  • overrides /G3, /G4, /G6, /GB and /D_M_IX86=anything

This option passes to both the front-end and back-end compilers (C1, C1XX and C2).
