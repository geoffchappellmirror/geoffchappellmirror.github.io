MSHTML 4.70 Ordinal-Only Exports

The earliest known MSHTML exports seven functions by ordinal only. Two didn't even survive to the second known build. None survived to the next version. Thereafter, all exports from MSHTML, whether documented or not, are by name until version 8.0.

Ordinal Remarks
100 discontinued in 4.71
101 discontinued in 4.71
102 discontinued in 4.71
103 discontinued in 4.71
104 discontinued in 4.71
105 discontinued in 4.70 from Windows 95 OSR2
106 discontinued in 4.70 from Windows 95 OSR2
