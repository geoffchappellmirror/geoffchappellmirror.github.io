Valediction

Version 6.2 discontinues some exports:

  • GetProcessUserModeExceptionPolicy
  • IsTimeZoneRedirectionEnabled
  • RegKrnGetGlobalState
  • RegKrnInitialize
  • SetClientTimeZoneInformation
  • SetProcessUserModeExceptionPolicy
