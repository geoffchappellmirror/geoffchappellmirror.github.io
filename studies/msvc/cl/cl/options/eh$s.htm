CL /EHs

Syntax

positive:

/EHs

negative:

/EHs-

Relations

The positive and negative forms override each other, of course. Additionally, /EHs in its positive form

  • weakly implies /D_CPPUNWIND for the front-end C++ compiler (C1XX)
  • overrides /EHa

and /EHs- overrides /D_CPPUNWIND.

The positive form passes to the front-end and back-end compilers for C++ only (C1XX and C2 respectively). The negative form does not pass to any compiler module.
