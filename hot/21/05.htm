Most Viewed in May 2021

This site had 18,451 visits in May 2021 from 12,913 unique visitors. That’s 595 visits per day. Collectively, they downloaded 79,303 pages.

This is a small drop from the month before. I’d be disappointed, except that it’s not as if I’ve written anything new of substance in a while, having instead attended mostly to website design both too much and not yet enough.

The list below is of document pages that were each viewed at least 100 times in the month. Ranks in parentheses are from April 2021. Faded titles are just index pages which I presume are viewed only or mainly on the way to others, especially while moving from one Table of Contents (TOC) to another. One of those index pages is just the skimpiest of placeholders, pending my writing an introduction, which I likely never will get round to. The TOCs are omitted entirely, as is the banner page, since none of these are meant to be seen independently of a document page.
