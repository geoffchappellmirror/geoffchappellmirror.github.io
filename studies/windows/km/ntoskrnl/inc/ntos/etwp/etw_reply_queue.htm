ETW_REPLY_QUEUE

The ETW_REPLY_QUEUE structure is 0x2C and 0x48 bytes in 32-bit and 64-bit Windows, respectively.

Offset (x86) Offset (x64) Definition Versions
0x00 0x00 
KQUEUE Queue;
 6.0 and higher
0x28 0x40 
LONG EventsLost;
 6.0 and higher
