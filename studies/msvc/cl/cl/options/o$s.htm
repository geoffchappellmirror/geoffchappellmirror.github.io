CL /Os

This option enables optimisations that save space.

Syntax

The option is /Os only.

Relations

The /Os option

  • overrides /O and /Ot, and is overridden by them
  • is weakly implied by /O1
  • is weakly overridden by /O2, /Od and /Ox
