CL Command-Line Warning D4025

Message Text 

overriding '/option1' with '/option2'

Circumstances

The two options shown are each given explicitly on the command line, /option1 preceding /option2. Among the syntactic relations for /option2 is that /option2 overrides at least one generated option that is implied (strongly) by /option1.

The two options are each shown with their arguments, if any, such as CL has interpreted them.

This warning is suppressed by the /noover option.
