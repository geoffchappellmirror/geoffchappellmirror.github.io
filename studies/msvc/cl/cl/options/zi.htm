CL /ZI

Syntax

The option is /ZI only.

Relations

The /ZI option

  • weakly implies /FC, /FD, /GF, /Gy and /Zi
  • weakly implies /debug for the linker
  • overrides /Z7 and /Zd, and is overridden by them
  • is incompatible with /clr, /dlp, /GL, /O1, /O2, /Ob1, /Ob2, /Og and /Ox
  • is among the prerequisites for /Gm

This option passes to both the front-end and back-end compilers (C1, C1XX and C2).
